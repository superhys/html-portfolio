The Best Games According to Rhys Elliott

My top 3 video games of the 2020s.

The Last of Us: Part II

A beautiful narrative cocktail of environmental storytelling, complex character relationships, and the cycle of violence.

Hades

A vibrant art style, fluid gameplay, and a delf handling of narrative in a die-retry rogue-like game.

Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 1+2

A faithful remake of a game that changed my life as a kid. Features the killer gameplay, soundtrack, and skate culture I fell in love with.
